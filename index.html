Welcome to the AIML StudyConnect, an all in one solution
Access all the course-related content under the Course tab
View all the announcements from various sources under the Feed tab

View the important dates including festivals, events, official holidays etc. through the Academic Calendar tab in the menu
View the important sheets and form including academic calendars, details in Important Docs
